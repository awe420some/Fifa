## Architektur — Aktueller Stand

### Modelle

- **Predictor**: Elo + Dixon-Coles + Squad-Delta + Market-Consensus + Covariates (Travel/TZ/Rest/Climate)
- **Monte-Carlo**: 25k Iter default, 8k für Player-Tracking
- **Per-Player**: Poisson-Approx `P(scores) = 1 − exp(−λ × p_player)`
- **Match-Forecasts**: alle 104 Matches inkl. KO mit Slot-Resolution (`1A`, `3CEFHI`, `W73`, `L101`)
